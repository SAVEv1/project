About Us

S.A.V.E- Saving And Valuing the Earth is an innovative initiative designed to promote sustainability and ethical waste management. Our platform aims to educate and engage individuals and communities in Bahrain and beyond, encouraging them to adopt eco-friendly practices and make informed decisions about recycling and waste reduction.

Our Story

S.A.V.E. was developed out of a passion for sustainability, founded at Bahrain Polytechnic by a passionate team:Yusuf Eid, Reema Noor, Nawraa Nama, Tasneem Alhayki, and Akram Aldahyani. We recognized the pressing challenges of recycling and waste management and the need for engaging educational resources. With a shared vision, we developed a dynamic interactive web application designed to empower users on their eco-friendly journeys.

Our Mission

At S.A.V.E., our mission is to promote Sustainable Development Goal 12 by connecting the gap between knowledge and action in recycling and waste management. We provide accessible and engaging educational resources that educate individuals with effective recycling strategies and waste reduction techniques. Through our interactive platform, we aim to inspire collective action toward a more sustainable future, enabling every user to take informed steps toward a greener planet.

What We Offer

  • Educational Resources Easy-to-understand guides on recycling and waste management practices.
  • Gamified Challenges: Fun activities where you can track your recycling efforts, earn points, and win rewards.
  • Marketplace for Reusables A space to trade reusable items, promoting less waste
  • Art Gallery A curated space showcasing creative works that promote sustainability and environmental awareness.

Our Vision

We envision a world in which sustainable habits become the standard. By adding innovative features to our platform, we strive to increase user engagement and develop a community dedicated to environmental awareness. We welcome you to join us on this journey to sustainability, where every action helps to create a healthy planet. Together, we can change the way we think about waste and promote a culture of sustainability that benefits everyone.
